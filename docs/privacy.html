Privacy Policy — Hajj Proximity

Last updated: July 7, 2026

Hajj Proximity is a free app that alerts pilgrims when they approach a Meeqat boundary or enter/exit the Haram sanctuary around Makkah. This policy explains what data the app uses and why.

In short: your location never leaves your device. There is no account, no server, and no backend for this app — all data stays on your phone.

Location data

The app uses your device's GPS location (including in the background, when tracking is enabled) to calculate your distance to the five fixed Meeqat points and whether you are inside the Haram boundary. This calculation happens entirely on your device.

  • Your raw location is never stored or transmitted anywhere.
  • Only small derived flags are saved locally on your device (in app storage, not on a server): which Meeqat points you've already been alerted for, whether you were last inside or outside the Haram, and your chosen alert distance. This lets the app avoid repeating an alert you've already received.
  • Background location is used only to trigger Meeqat/Haram notifications while the app is closed. You can disable background tracking at any time from the Alerts tab.

Other data stored on your device

Your Ihram checklist progress and alert settings (thresholds, which alert types are enabled) are saved locally on your device only, so they persist between app sessions. None of this is sent anywhere.

Map tiles

The map screens load map imagery from Esri's tile service and, on Android, use the Google Maps SDK to render the map. Loading a map tile works like loading any web page: the tile provider can see your device's IP address and which map area you're viewing, the same way any website sees visitor requests. No location coordinates or personal data are sent to these providers beyond what's needed to fetch the visible map area.

Crash reporting

The app may use Sentry to automatically report crashes and errors, so problems can be fixed. If enabled, this sends technical diagnostic data (such as a stack trace, app version, and general device information) to Sentry. This data is used only for fixing bugs and is not linked to your identity or location.

What we don't do

  • No user accounts or sign-in
  • No advertising or ad tracking
  • No analytics that identify individual users
  • No selling or sharing of data with third parties for marketing
  • No access to contacts, camera, microphone, or photos

Permissions the app requests

  • Location (foreground and background): required for proximity alerts, as described above.
  • Notifications: required to alert you when you approach a Meeqat or cross the Haram boundary.

Children's privacy

This app is not directed at children and does not knowingly collect data from children.
